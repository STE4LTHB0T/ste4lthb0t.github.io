Work

Otaku Nadu, Chennai

Otaku Nadu, a social media page managed by a team of 15 people who are dedicated to bring together people who are fond of Japanese culture. When it comes to Japanese culture, we are not only limited to bring together people who are fond of Japanese anime, but we are also dedicated to bring together people who are fond of the Japanese Music, Tourism, the Automobile culture and many more.

As a member of Otaku Nadu, I specialize in upscaling images and videos of anime and other related works using pre-trained machine learning models. While working at Otaku Nadu, I created a Discord bot which can be used for various purposes of Discord servers. Besides that, I specialize in content creation, streaming and helping in event management.

Spike#8670

Spike#8670, a Discord bot that I started to work on since 2020 as a hobby with friends, which is now a well-developed bot used for moderation of servers and many more. The name of the bot, "Spike", is a reference to Director Shinichir≈ç Watanabe's popular work of the main characater of the show "Cowboy Bebop" - Spike Spiegel.

A notable feature of the bot is the Profile of users. The bot was developed as a hobby first in a gaming server. When it comes to gaming, gamers play on different platforms and different games. To add friends, a profile feature was created, where gamers would enter information about the game and the username which gets stored in a MongoDB, a cloud database. Anybody can search up for the user and obtain the profile document of the desired user. A similar method was also used for the warnings of users in servers.

Another feature of the bot is the Economy commands. Users in the server obtain XP (levels) & Woolongs (currency used in the world of Cowboy Bebop) based on their activity in the server. The currency obtained by the users can be used to do bounty hunts in various planets in the world of Cowboy Bebop. Users who wants to become bounty hunters can also do their part using various commands that are linked with Economy.
